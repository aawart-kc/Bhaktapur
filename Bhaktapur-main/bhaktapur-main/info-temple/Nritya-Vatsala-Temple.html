Nritya-Vatsala Temple

The Vatsala Durga Temple, also known as the Nritya Vatsala Temple, in Bhaktapur Durbar Square, was a stone temple dedicated to the goddess Vatsala Durga, a form of the Hindu goddess Durga. It was constructed by King Jagat Prakash Malla in the late 17th century, with modifications and additions by later kings, particularly King Bhupatindra Malla.
